Feature Guide
Hide Soft

Filters out soft-failed torrents from the results. Soft failures are temporary tracker issues (timeouts, DNS errors) that typically resolve automatically. Only hard failures (unregistered, deleted, banned) will be shown.

Also Delete Files

When enabled, deletes both the torrent and all downloaded files from disk. When disabled, only removes the torrent from your client while keeping the files intact.
